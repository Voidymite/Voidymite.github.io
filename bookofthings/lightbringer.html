The Lightbringer

The Lightbringer's appearance
Status: Alive

Looks

The Lightbringer is a white man wearing a tee shirt and tie overlayed by a black tanktop, donning a black fedora.

Description

THE LIGHTBRINGER!!! is an incredibly powerful being who wears his ICONIC magical fedora. Some theorize that at one point he may have been a nice being who helped people, but this has been debated. he is currently known for committing various crimes like breaking into YOUR HOUSE SPECIFICALLY and STEALING YOUR OATS. He can run at high speeds and flies around in his fedora shaped spaceship.

Trivia

  • THE LIGHTBRINGER!!!'s catchphrase is "Nobody outruns The Lightbringer!!!"
  • The best defense against THE LIGHTBRINGER!!! is to place a rake on the ground near your oats. this will cause him to step on the rake and will leave him dazed with two tiny birds flying around his head. At this moment you can steal his fedora and feed it to worms.

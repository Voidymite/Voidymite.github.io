Duck vs Bread

Controls & Power-ups

Controls:

  • 🎮 Arrow Keys / A,D - Move left/right
  • 🏃 Hold Shift - Run faster
  • ⬆️ Z - Jump (press twice to double jump)
  • 💫 Hold X - Activate powerup

Power-ups:

  • 🔥 Flamethrower - Burn bread in front of you
  • 🍽️ Eat Power - Instantly eat bread on contact
  • 💨 Fan - Push bread away from you
  • 🥚 Eggs - Throw eggs that target the nearest bread
  • 🐤 Mini Duck - Become smaller and jump higher
  • 💣 Bombs - Drop bombs that explode on contact with bread
  • ⚔️ Sword - Throw a spinning sword that slices through bread

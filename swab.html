Swab's Expedition is a fangame of indie game Pizza Tower by Tour de Pizza here a little cotton guy traverses the depths of a mysterious spire!

"Run around and have fun!"

Run through silly levels with goofy challenges for your little guy while screaming "Awwww!" at how chibi he looks!

Yikes! Avoid the evil Swabble and make sure your time doesn't run out... or else!

Considered arguably one of the first actually original Pizza Tower fangames!

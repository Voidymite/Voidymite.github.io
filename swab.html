Swab Meldaro Theodore is a cotton creature who resides in an unspecified desert on Earth. He’s an adventure type and carries a backpack where-ever he goes (kinda like a Belly Bag from U.G thing). One day, he wakes up to mail on his face. He reads the note to find out he got invited to an unknown building called “The Swab”. Up for the challenge, Swab locates the spire to find what resides in it…

One step for cotton, one... um... something... for someone..

Swab's Expedition is a fangame of indie game Pizza Tower by Tour de Pizza here a little cotton guy traverses the depths of a mysterious spire!

"Run around and have fun!"

Run through silly levels with goofy challenges for your little guy while screaming "Awwww!" at how chibi he looks!

Yikes! Avoid the evil Swabble and make sure your time doesn't run out... or else!

Considered arguably one of the first actually original Pizza Tower fangames!
